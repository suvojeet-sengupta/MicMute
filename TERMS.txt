==============================================================================
                               DESCRIPTION
==============================================================================

MicMute-S is a high-performance, professional-grade microphone control utility 
designed specifically for Windows 10 and 11. 

KEY FEATURES:
* System-Wide Mute: Instantly mute your microphone globally, affecting all apps 
  (Zoom, Teams, Discord, etc.) simultaneously.
* Force Mute Mode: Prevents applications from unmuting your microphone without 
  your permission.
* Visual Feedback: Clear overlay and tray icons so you always know your status.
* Push-to-Talk: Configurable hotkeys for seamless audio control.
* Privacy First: Your audio data stays on your device.
